📖 Past Simple – עבר פשוט
Unit 3 · A Look at Language (pages 9–10)

What is it? / מה זה?

We use the Past Simple for actions that started and finished in the past.

משתמשים בעבר פשוט לפעולות שקרו והסתיימו בעבר.

Last night, we finished all our food.  ·  A young girl brought them an onion.

⏰ Time Expressions: yesterday · אתמול  |  last night/week/year · אמש / שבוע / שנה שעברו  |  ago · לפני

Regular Verbs – פעלים סדירים

  • Most verbs: add -ed → cook → cooked (בישל)
  • Ending in -e: add -d → arrive → arrived (הגיע)
  • Consonant+y: change to -ied → try → tried (ניסה)
  • Short CVC: double consonant → stop → stopped (עצר)

Questions & Negatives – שאלות ושלילה

Use did / didn't + base form.

Did the girl bring them a potato?  ·  No, she didn't bring them a potato.
