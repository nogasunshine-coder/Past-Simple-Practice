📖 Past Simple – עבר פשוט
Unit 3 · A Look at Language (pages 9–10)

What is it? / מה זה?

We use the Past Simple to talk about actions and events that started and finished in the past.

משתמשים בעבר פשוט לתיאור פעולות שקרו והסתיימו בעבר.


Last night, we finished all our food.

A young girl brought them an onion.

⏰ Time Expressions: yesterday · last night / week / year · two years ago · in 2020

Regular Verbs – פעלים סדירים

  • Most verbs: add -ed → cook → cooked, play → played
  • Verbs ending in -e: add -d → arrive → arrived
  • Verbs ending in consonant+y: change to -ied → try → tried
  • Short CVC verbs: double the consonant → stop → stopped

Questions & Negatives – שאלות ושלילה

In questions and negatives, we use the helper verb did / didn't.
The main verb stays in its base form!

Did the girl bring them a potato?

No, she didn't. She didn't bring them a potato.
